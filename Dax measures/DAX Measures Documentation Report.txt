📊 DAX Measures & KPI Documentation
This project features a centralized library of Calculated Measures designed to provide deep business insights across multiple domains: Sales, Marketing, and Support.

1. Retail Sales & Profitability
This group handles the core revenue logic, utilizing dynamic pricing to ensure accuracy across millions of records.

Total Revenue

Purpose: Calculates gross income by matching quantities with time-specific prices.

Logic: Uses SUMX and LOOKUPVALUE to fetch the correct price from the PRICE_CHANGE table based on the Year/Week context.

Total Quantity

Purpose: Summarizes the total number of units sold.

Avg Selling Price (AOV)

Purpose: Determines the actual average price per unit sold.

Logic: DIVIDE([Total Revenue], [Total Quantity], 0).

Revenue YTD (Year-to-Date)

Purpose: Tracks cumulative sales performance from the start of the current year.

Logic: TOTALYTD([Total Revenue], 'ORDER'[order_date]).

2. Growth & Time Intelligence
Essential for management to track performance trends over time.

Revenue LM (Last Month)

Purpose: Acts as a baseline for monthly performance comparisons.

Sales Growth MoM%

Purpose: Measures the percentage increase or decrease in sales compared to the previous month.

Logic: DIVIDE([Total Revenue] - [Revenue LM], [Revenue LM], 0).

YoY Growth %

Purpose: Compares current performance against the same period last year to account for seasonality.

3. Customer & Ranking Insights
Measures designed to analyze purchasing power and product performance.

Total Customers: Counts unique customers using DISTINCTCOUNT.

Revenue per Customer: Calculates the average lifetime value per unique user.

Product Sales Rank

Purpose: Dynamically ranks products based on revenue.

Logic: RANKX(ALL('PRODUCT'), [Total Revenue], , DESC).

Category Contribution %

Purpose: Shows how much each category represents of the total sales (Dynamic based on Slicers).

4. Marketing Performance
Analyzes over 800,000 interactions from the CAMPAIGN_TOUCH table.

Total Marketing Touches: Total volume of marketing interactions.

Campaign Conversion Rate

Purpose: Measures campaign success in converting leads to outcomes.

Logic: CALCULATE(COUNTROWS(CAMPAIGN_TOUCH), CAMPAIGN_TOUCH[outcome] = "1") / [Total Marketing Touches].

5. Customer Support & External Context
Avg Satisfaction Score (CSAT): Average rating from the support team.

Pending Tickets Count: Tracks unresolved issues where status is not "Resolved".

Avg Temperature: Correlates sales trends with environmental data from the EXTERNAL_FACTOR table.

💡 Developer Notes
Centralized Measures: All logic is stored in the _Key_Measures table for professional organization.

Global Safe Division: The DIVIDE function is used across all metrics to handle nulls and prevent "Division by Zero" errors.

Dynamic Formatting: * Currency: Applied to all Revenue/Price metrics.

Percentage: Applied to Growth and Conversion metrics.

Whole Numbers: Applied to Counts and Quantities.
