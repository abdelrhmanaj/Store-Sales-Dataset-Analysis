1. Retail Sales Metrics
This group handles the core revenue logic, utilizing dynamic pricing based on historical changes.

Total Revenue

Purpose: Calculates gross income by matching each product's quantity with its specific price for the given Year and Week.

Logic: Uses SUMX to iterate through orders and LOOKUPVALUE to fetch the correct price from the PRICE_CHANGE table.

Total Quantity

Purpose: Summarizes the total number of units sold across all transactions.

Avg Selling Price

Purpose: Determines the actual average price per unit sold.

Logic: DIVIDE([Total Revenue], [Total Quantity], 0).

2. Customer Insights
Measures designed to analyze purchasing power and customer base growth.

Total Customers

Purpose: Counts unique customers who have placed at least one order.

Revenue per Customer

Purpose: Calculates the Average Order Value (AOV) per unique user.

Logic: Divides Total Revenue by the count of unique Customer IDs.

3. Marketing Performance
Derived from the CAMPAIGN_TOUCH table, analyzing over 800,000 interactions.

Total Marketing Touches

Purpose: Counts the total number of marketing interactions recorded.

Campaign Conversion Rate

Purpose: Measures the effectiveness of campaigns in converting leads.

Logic: Uses CALCULATE to count rows where Outcome = 1, divided by the total marketing touches.

4. Customer Support Analysis
Focuses on service quality using data from the SUPPORT_TICKET table.

Avg Satisfaction Score (CSAT)

Purpose: Measures the average customer satisfaction rating.

Pending Tickets Count

Purpose: Tracks unresolved issues (where status is not 'Resolved').

5. Environmental & External Context
Avg Temperature

Purpose: Calculates the average temperature from the EXTERNAL_FACTOR table to study its correlation with sales trends.

💡 Developer Notes
Centralized Storage: All measures are stored in the _Key_Measures calculated table for easy access.

Safe Division: The DIVIDE function is used globally to prevent "Division by Zero" errors.

Data Formatting: Revenue is formatted as Currency, and Conversion Rate as Percentage for immediate use in visualizations.
